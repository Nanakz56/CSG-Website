Datasets

Curated Collection of Socially-Relevant Datasets

Heart Disease

This Kaggle.com publication presents data on risk factors related to heart disease. One of our projects uses this data to develop a model that informs the user if they are at risk for contracting this illness.

Food Access

Acquired from the United States Department of Agriculture, this data set contains information about the US population and varying distances from residential areas to the closest grocery store.

Energy Consumption

This expansive data set from the US Energy Information Administration contains data on several energy production methods, the consumption by commercial industries, and the cost associated with the entire process.

Housing

Housing-related data compiled and published by the United States Census Bureau from the year 2020. This large collection provides insights into key demographics of households by race, age, size, etc.

Government Spending

Provided by DataOhio, this data set showcases the latest distribution of government funds to various agencies and operations.

Crime Rates

Curated by the United States Justice Department and other federal agencies, this data set provides key insights into crime rates across the country. Ranging from robberies to assaults, there is a lot to uncover.

Lead Concerns

Compiled by DataOhio.gov, this data set contains property information of residents who have refused or failed to comply with lead safety regulations, resulting in health concerns around these areas.
